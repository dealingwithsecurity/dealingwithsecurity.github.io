Dealing With Security

The Idea

Dealing With Security provides a simple model and recommended services to exchange information ands other goods on internet, with strong encryption and anonymity for both parties.

The Models

Cryptocat

Is an app or a browser plug-in that provides strong encryption for "Off-The-Record" conversations. Conversations are not kept, and you are not anonymous (it is strongly advised to use it trough Tor to gain anonymity). Use it like a chat service, for quick and ephemerous conversatios.

Tor Browser Bundle

The Onion Router (TOR) is a anonymising browser, that uses an untraceable cascade of proxies to give you a new identity online. Use it to surf the Deep Web, check your false email or login to your online wallet.

OpenMailBox

Is a web-based email provider. Using only open software, it has privacy in mind and will always be free. Use it to create a new identity with TOR. It is strongly advised to use PGP encryption for your emails.

Blockchain

Is the biggest bitcoin service, providing the blockchain explorer, multiple developers API and free, online wallets. Use your OpenMailBox email to create a wallet and you're set to make anonymous bitcoins transaction. The Blockchain also offer mobile-based apps, it is strongly advised not to use it, as your phone can be traced to you. Read Split Your Life In Two.

DuckDuckGo

The non-tracking search engine. Since 2009, DuckDuckGo provides anonymous (as in, not tracking you) google-like searching capability. More than that, they offer a clean interface, with almost no ads and a smart search algorithm. Use it trough Tor to search the Clean Web, like you use google.
